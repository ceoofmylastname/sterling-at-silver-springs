Spring Landscaping Schedule

Spring landscaping begins April 15. Common area plantings and irrigation maintenance over two weeks. Temporary path closures may occur near the main entrance. Residents are reminded to keep personal landscaping within community guidelines.

Welcome to Sterling at Silver Springs

Everything you need to know to get settled in your new home.

Congratulations on your new home! We're glad to have you as part of the community. This page is your one-stop guide to getting started.

New Resident Checklist

  • Set Up Utilities — Electric, Water/Sewer, Gas, Trash/Recycling, Internet
  • Contact the Property Manager — Introduce yourself, get mailbox key / gate access
  • Review Community Documents — CC&Rs, Rules & Regulations, Architectural Guidelines
  • Register Your Vehicle(s) — Parking registration, decals if applicable
  • Register Pets — Pet registration, leash rules, waste stations
  • Update Your Mailing Address — USPS, voter registration, driver's license
  • Join the Community — Bookmark this website, check Activities, subscribe to newsletter
