## Section 1: Page Header

**Heading:** Community Announcements

---

## Section 2: Announcement Template
> **ADMIN INSTRUCTIONS — How to Post a New Announcement:**
> 1. Click "Edit" on this page in Google Sites
> 2. Copy the template below
> 3. Paste it at the TOP of the announcements list (newest first)
> 4. Fill in the details
> 5. Attach a PDF if needed (drag and drop)
> 6. Click "Publish"

### Announcement Format Template:
```
---
**[Month Day, Year] — [Announcement Title]**

[One to three paragraphs of announcement text. Keep it clear and concise.
Include any action items or deadlines for residents.]

**Action Required:** [Yes/No — if yes, what residents need to do]
**Deadline:** [Date, if applicable]
**Questions?** Contact [name] at [email] or [Submit a Request].

[Optional: Attached PDF — click to download]
---
```

## Section 3: Announcements List
> **Google Sites instruction:** Each announcement is a text section with a divider line between entries. Newest at the top, oldest at the bottom. Archive announcements older than 12 months by moving them to a sub-page or deleting them.

### Sample Announcements:

---

**March 30, 2026 — Welcome to Our New Community Website!**

The Sterling at Silver Springs Board of Directors is proud to announce the launch of our new community website. Here you'll find meeting information, newsletters, community resources, and an easy way to submit requests directly to the board.

Bookmark this page and check back regularly for updates. If you have feedback about the site, please use our Suggestion Box.

**Action Required:** No
**Questions?** Contact the Board via the Contact Us page.

---

**March 28, 2026 — Spring Landscaping Schedule**

Spring landscaping begins April 15, 2026. Common area plantings and irrigation system maintenance will take place over a two-week period. Temporary path closures may occur near the main entrance.

Residents are reminded to keep personal landscaping within community guidelines. See the Resources page for the current Architectural & Landscaping Guidelines document.

**Action Required:** No — informational only
**Deadline:** N/A
**Questions?** Submit a Request under "Landscaping & Grounds."
