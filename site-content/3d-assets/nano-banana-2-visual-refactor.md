# Sterling at Silver Springs — Visual Refactor & Dimension Calibration
> **Engine:** Google Nano Banana 2
> **Purpose:** Generate correctly framed hero images optimized for ultra-wide desktop headers + mobile-first responsiveness

---

## 1. Dimensional & Framing Instructions

| Parameter | Value | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| **Aspect Ratio** | 21:9 (Ultra-wide) | Prevents "sky-only" or "tree-top" cropping on desktop headers |
| **Resolution** | 4K+ (3440×1440 minimum) | Crisp at all viewport sizes |
| **Vertical Center-Point** | Primary subject in **middle-third** vertically | House/pool/room stays visible whether cropped wide or tall |
| **Safe Zones** | 20% breathable space on left & right edges | Clean cropping when site collapses to portrait mobile view |
| **Format** | PNG master → WebP delivery | Lossless master, compressed web output |

## 3. Aesthetic Directives (Apply to ALL Prompts)

### Sharp-Focus Architecture
- Crisp lines, high contrast, deep saturated colors
- No softness, no haze, no dreamy effects
- Architectural detail must be razor-sharp (stucco texture, tile grooves, grout lines)

### Glass-Morphism Integration Zone
- Center 40% of each image should have subtly softer, more even lighting
- This creates a natural "frosted glass" landing spot for the website's text overlays
- Don't make it obvious — just slightly reduced contrast in the center band

### Color Synchronization
All images must use the community's canonical color palette:

| Color | Hex | Where It Appears |
|-------|-----|------------------|
| Primary Blue | #3D5A80 | Twilight/night tones |
| Warm Cream | #F7F3E9 | Stucco, trim, walls |
| Gold Accent | #D4A843 | Metallic lettering, warm light, sunset |
| Deep Navy | #2B2D42 | Night sky, deep shadows |
| Sage Green | #6B9080 | Desert shrubs, interior accents |
| Terracotta | #B85C38 | Roof tiles, earth accents |
| Desert Sand | #D4B896 | Decomposed granite, stone |
| Desert Blue Sky | #4A90C4 | Daytime sky — vibrant, clear |

## 4. Quality Control Checklist

- [ ] **21:9 aspect ratio** — no 16:9 or 4:3
- [ ] **Subject in center-third** vertically — not cropped to sky or ground
- [ ] **20% safe zones** on left/right edges — clean mobile crop
- [ ] **Desert-contemporary ONLY** — stucco + terracotta, no brick/colonial/craftsman
- [ ] **Xeriscape ONLY** — no green lawns, no lush gardens
- [ ] **4K+ resolution** — minimum 3440×1440
- [ ] **No people, no vehicles**
- [ ] **Correct spelling** — "Sterling at Silver Springs" on any visible signage
- [ ] **Sharp focus** — no blur, no AI artifacts, no uncanny valley
- [ ] **Center softness zone** — subtle lighting accommodation for text overlays
