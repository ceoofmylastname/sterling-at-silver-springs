# Veo Animation Prompt — "The Restoration Loop"
> Sterling at Silver Springs Community Website
> 5-second cinematic 3D animation for community branding and hero section
> **Architectural DNA**: Desert-contemporary. Sand-beige stucco. Terracotta clay barrel tile roofs. Xeriscape landscaping. Desert blue skies.

---

## Primary Prompt (Veo Format)

```
A 5-second cinematic 3D camera pan of a desert-contemporary residential community entrance monument sign reading "Sterling at Silver Springs."

The monument is a horizontal stucco structure with a terracotta clay barrel tile cap, flanked by desert xeriscape landscaping and tall date palms.

The shot begins on the LEFT side of the monument in a NEGLECTED state:
- The sand-beige stucco is cracked, sun-bleached, and peeling with visible water stains
- Metal lettering is tarnished, oxidized, and dull — partially obscured by dried desert vine tendrils
- Xeriscape beds are ruined: dead ornamental grasses, scattered gravel washed away, tumbleweeds caught against the base
- Terracotta tile cap has cracked and missing tiles, exposing the substrate
- A decorative lamp post nearby leans slightly with a broken glass globe, light off
- The stone-gray asphalt road is cracked with faded lane markings and sand drift across the surface
- Date palms show dead fronds hanging, unmanicured
- Color palette is desaturated: bleached-out sand, faded terracotta, dusty gray-brown
- Hazy, washed-out desert sky with no vibrancy

As the camera SLOWLY PANS RIGHT across the monument (smooth dolly motion, eye level), a "cleansing" transformation sweeps across the frame from left to right:
- A warm golden light wave passes across the scene like a desert sunrise washing over the landscape
- Where the light touches, everything RESTORES:
  - Stucco repairs and brightens to clean sand-beige with smooth finish
  - Metal lettering polishes to gleaming brushed steel with gold accents
  - Dead xeriscape dissolves into fresh landscaping: clean decomposed granite, purple fountain grass, blue fescue, flowering desert sage, accent boulders
  - Terracotta tiles restore — rich reddish-brown, uniform, perfectly aligned
  - Cracked asphalt smooths into fresh stone-gray road with crisp white markings
  - The lamp post straightens, globe repairs, illuminates with warm amber light
  - Palm trees: dead fronds vanish, fresh green fronds fan outward
  - Desert sand drift clears away

The shot ENDS on the RIGHT side of the monument in its PRISTINE state:
- Perfect desert Golden Hour lighting, warm directional sun low on the horizon
- Every element is immaculate: gleaming lettering, clean stucco, manicured xeriscape
- Vibrant desert blue sky overhead with wispy clouds catching golden light
- The terracotta tile cap glows warm in the sunset
- Distant mountain silhouettes visible on the horizon
- Subtle lens flare as the camera settles on the final composition

Camera motion: Smooth continuous dolly pan, LEFT to RIGHT, eye level (approximately 4 feet height), steady and cinematic. No handheld shake. The "cleansing wave" travels at roughly the same speed as the camera pan, keeping the transition line near center frame.

Atmosphere: The transformation should feel magical but grounded — not fantasy sparkles, but a convincing "time-lapse of restoration" condensed into the sweep of golden light. The emotional arc goes from "neglected desert ruin" to "pristine desert-contemporary community pride."

Style: Photorealistic 3D render. Cinematic aspect ratio (2.39:1). 24fps. Professional color grading with the warm/cool contrast between the neglected (bleached, flat) and restored (warm, saturated) states. Desert color palette throughout.
```

## Loop Specification

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Duration | 5 seconds (120 frames at 24fps) |
| Resolution | 4K (3840x2160) minimum |
| Aspect Ratio | 2.39:1 (cinematic widescreen) or 16:9 |
| Frame Rate | 24fps (cinematic) |
| Format | MP4 (H.265), WebM for web fallback |
| Loop Method | **Seamless ping-pong** — plays forward (neglected → pristine), then reverses (pristine → neglected), creating a continuous 10-second loop |
| Audio | None (website will overlay ambient audio or play silent) |

## Color Grading Reference

### Neglected State (0s–1s)
- **Dominant tones:** Bleached sand, faded terracotta, dusty gray
- **Shadows:** Warm gray-brown (#6B6558)
- **Midtones:** Washed-out sand (#C4B8A0)
- **Highlights:** Bleached off-white (#E8E0D0)
- **Saturation:** 25% of normal (sun-bleached desert)
- **Contrast:** Low, flat, hazy

### Transition Wave
- **Wave color:** Warm golden (#D4A843 at 60% opacity)
- **Wave edge:** Soft gradient, not a hard line — approximately 15% of frame width
- **Sparkle/particles:** Subtle golden dust motes caught in the light — not magical, but atmospheric desert particulate

### Pristine State (4s–5s)
- **Dominant tones:** Warm sand, rich terracotta, vibrant desert blue
- **Shadows:** Deep terracotta-brown (#6B3A20)
- **Midtones:** Clean sand-beige (#D4B896), sage green (#6B9080)
- **Highlights:** Golden amber (#D4A843), clean stucco cream (#F7F3E9)
- **Saturation:** 115% of normal (vivid desert color)
- **Contrast:** High, cinematic, clear desert air

## Web Integration Notes

- **Hero section:** Embed as auto-playing, muted, looping `<video>` behind hero text with `object-fit: cover`
- **Poster frame:** Use the final pristine frame (5.0s) as the `poster` attribute and fallback image
- **Performance:** Provide both MP4 (H.265) and WebM; lazy-load below the fold
- **Reduced motion:** If `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`, show the poster image instead of video
- **Dark mode:** The animation works in both themes — the pristine golden hour desert lighting contrasts beautifully against dark UI elements
- **Glass-morphism overlay:** Apply a subtle frosted glass border treatment to the video container to bridge photorealistic imagery with the site's modern UI
